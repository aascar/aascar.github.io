• A self paced learning community

We are a group of self paced learners who collaborated to work on cutting edge technologies. We just work in our free time and contribute to any project intended to solve real world problems using Computer Science technologies.


Objectives:

  • Self driven learning of trending technologies.
  • Collaborated learning with hands on experience (projects).
  • Developing products with Industry standards.
  • Improving our coding skills.
  • Covering all aspects of Software Development Life Cycle.

Some Informal Key Points

  • We love Computer Science
  • We have Teacher, Team Leader, Project Manager
  • We work whenever we want
  • We share our breakthrough ideas
  • We are ready to implement our ideas
  • We create projects and allow collaborative development
  • We recreate existing projects with new technologies
  • We are always open to learn and also to teach
  • Everything open-source.

We don't have any office or location to stay together, but our ideas do. Since we are a open-source community our projects are available for all to use and add features. We welcome every technology enthusiast to be a part our community.
